# Digital Twin-Driven Strategic Demolition Plan for Circular Asset Management of Bridge Infrastructures

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a simulation model based on the concept of a **Digital Twin** applied to bridge infrastructure systems. A digital twin is a virtual representation of a physical asset that continuously monitors, analyzes, and predicts its performance over time.

The objective of this project is to develop a decision-support system that assists in **strategic demolition planning** and promotes **circular asset management** in bridge infrastructure.

This work is inspired by recent research in lifecycle engineering and smart infrastructure systems.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Model bridge infrastructure using digital twin concepts  
- Analyze lifecycle cost and carbon emissions  
- Evaluate structural health and condition over time  
- Support decision-making for demolition planning  
- Promote sustainable and circular infrastructure management  

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts Used

- Life Cycle Cost Analysis (LCCA)

## ⚙️ Features of the Model

### 1. Bridge Digital Twin Model
The system creates a virtual representation of bridges including:
- Age and design life
- Material and geometry
- Location and type

---

### 2. Life Cycle Cost Analysis
The model computes:
- Construction cost  
- Maintenance cost  
- Demolition cost  
- Total lifecycle cost  

---

### 3. Carbon Emission Analysis
The system evaluates environmental impact through:
- Construction emissions  
- Operational emissions  
- Demolition emissions  

---

### 4. Condition Assessment
A mathematical model is used to simulate structural degradation over time:
- Condition score (0–100)
- Health classification (Excellent → Critical)

---

### 5. Demolition Priority Planning
Based on remaining life and condition:
- High Priority  
- Medium Priority  
- Low Priority  

---

### 6. Visualization Dashboard
The system generates:
- Condition vs Time graphs  
- Cost analysis charts  
- Carbon emission breakdown  
- Multi-bridge comparison  

---
